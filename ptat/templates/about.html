Who We Are

Founded in 2011, Prabhu Tour & Travels was created with one clear vision — to put customer comfort above everything else. What started as a small initiative in Ludhiana has today become a trusted name across Punjab’s travel industry. With 20+ years of experienced professional drivers, we ensure every journey is not just safe, but smooth, relaxing, and memorable. From short city rides to long-distance tours, we focus on the little details that turn your ride into a “wow” experience.
